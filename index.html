About Champion Auto Center

Champion Auto Center Your Trusted Collision Repair and Full-Service Auto Shop.

At Champion Auto Center, we've been proudly serving the community for over 10 years, delivering high-quality collision repair, mechanical services, and auto painting. Our shop is equipped with state-of-the-art technology, including a professional paint booth and two types of frame machines, ensuring that your vehicle is restored to its original condition with precision and care. Our team consists of trained and licensed technicians who are experts in all aspects of auto repair and restoration. Whether it's repairing collision damage, performing full mechanical services, or providing a flawless paint job, we take pride in every job we do. We understand the importance of getting you back on the road safely and quickly. That's why we work hard to deliver efficient, reliable service that meets the highest industry standards.
